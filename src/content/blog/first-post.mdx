---
 title: "How to Spot a Hidden Leak"
 description: "Simple signs and steps to prevent water damage."
 date: "2024-06-01"
 tags: ["plumbing", "leaks"]
---

Finding leaks early helps avoid costly repairs. Look for damp patches, musty smells, and unexpected meter changes.

- Check under sinks and around toilets
- Inspect ceilings below bathrooms
- Watch your boiler pressure for drops

If in doubt, call a professional to assess and fix the issue safely.
